### `image-analysis dedupe`

| Option | Default |
|--------|---------|
| **day_directory** | (required) |
| **`--threshold`** | `0.65` (min cosine similarity for “same scene”) |

| **`--list-remove`** | off |
| **`--verbose` / `-v`** | off |

1. **Partial or interrupted download** (network drop, sleep, kill during first load).
2. **Corrupt cache** after an upgrade or disk issue.
